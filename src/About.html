About Us

Our History

Founded in 2017, Key Power Engineering Ltd is a building services engineering company that offers a wide array of expertise solutions that range from conception to commissioning in MEP services. The extent and quality of services provided by the company have helped accumulate high excellence in engineering services in Rwanda We believe in engineering solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients' technical skills and professional reputations.

Consequently, Key Power Engineering Ltd has grown to become a leading member in the field to better deliver our services and give a personalized experience to each person that crosses our threshold. At Key Power Engineering Ltd, we pride ourselves on combining facility engineering, energy management, and project management services, that enable us to deliver sustainable solutions for our clients and in turn reduce the carbon footprint of our output throug h our technical know-how and extensive expertise.
